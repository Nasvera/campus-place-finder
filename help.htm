Online Help:

The Campus Place Finder application helps employees, students, or visitors locate a person or a place within a campus, office building, military base or university. This application is typically used by employees, students or visitors on a campus.

To locate a person, select the Find Person radio button then simply enter the person's first name, last name or full name in the search box. Or to locate a place, select the Find Place radio button then simply enter the office or conference room number in the search box. The person's office or room of interest will then be highlighted on the map and relevant information about the space will be presented to you.

Using the Application

The Campus Place Finder application provides employees, students, and visitors an easy way to find people or places near them, a person by name, or room number. When you enter a name or room number into the search box, the application will zoom you to an exact match or list the closest matches to your search inquiry. View people and places near your location and allow you to review relevant location information in and around your campus.

Finding an Area of Interest

There are two primary ways to identify an area of interest. You can interact directly with the map and click on an office space or you can also use the search box to find a person or place.

Map Based Navigation

There are several ways to interact with the map:

1. Use the basemap switcher to select the basemap you'd like to interact with
2. Hold down the shift key and drag a box on the map to zoom in
3. Hold Down Ctrl+Shift to zoom out
4. Roll your mouse roller to zoom in/out
5. Drag the zoom slider to zoom in/out
6. Click on an interior space and see additional information about the space

Searching

The search box can be used to find a person or a place, you're interested in locating

1. Use the radio dial to select the search method you'd like to use Find Person or Find Place
2. Enter the person's, first name, last name or full name or the room number you're interested in
3. The application will zoom to any exact match, or supply a dropdown list of similar matches. Click on the person or place listed and the application will zoom to your selection.

More Information

The Campus Place Finder application can used by businesses, universities, military bases, facilities departments or other local government organizations responsible for interior spaces to deploy a web-based Campus Finder Application. This application provides 24x7 access to the organization and typically improves the efficiency numbers staffed by these organizations.

Please visit Esri's ArcGIS for Local Government Resource Center for additional information.

Browser Compatibility

The Campus Place Finder is supported on the following browsers: Microsoft IE 7/8/9, Mozilla Firefox, Google Chrome, and Apple Safari.
